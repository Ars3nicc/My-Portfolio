What is CAAP?
Civil Aviation Authority of the Philippines
Civil Aviation Authority of the Philippines - CANSO CAAP is responsible for the regulation of civil aviation in the Philippines. The CAAP offices that performs the regulation functions are Flight Standard and Inspectorate Service (FSIS) and Aerodrome and Air Navigation Services Oversight Office (AANSOO).
